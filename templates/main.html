General Decision Support Engine

Scope of the Project

The scope of this research paper is to provide a comprehensive analysis of General Decision Support Engines (G+DEs) and their role in supporting decision-making processes. The paper will cover the key components of G+DEs, including ontology-based knowledge representation, the process-type library, and forward completion. Additionally, the paper will explore the various applications of G+DEs in different fields, such as business, healthcare, and the military. The goal of this research is to provide insights into the potential of G+DEs as a tool for improving decision-making processes and to identify areas for future research and development.
